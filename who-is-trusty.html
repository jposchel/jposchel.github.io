Get to Know Trusty and Friends

Trusty and Friends is a group of folks who value the dental health and oral well-being of kids everywhere. Our goal is healthy teeth for every child.

Mission

Trusty and Friends empowers children and their parents and caregivers to engage at a young age in habits and activities that support sustained dental health.

Purpose

Trusty and Friends is a fresh dental health campaign crafted especially for kids, parents, and caregivers. We believe all kids deserve quality dental health. Establishing positive dental care habits early on in a child’s life is essential for them to enjoy positive dental health later in their adulthood.

Experience a renewed, re-imagined approach to the traditional instruction and motivation of children in positive dental care habits.

Hooray for Healthy Teeth!

All kids deserve healthy teeth! Trusty Tooth and his friends are tooth-care experts. They love helping kids keep their teeth in tip-top shape. It’s fun and rewarding to take care of your teeth, gums, and mouth. Let Trusty and Friends show you how to keep your teeth healthy and happy!
